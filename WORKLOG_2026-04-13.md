## 현재 `.ioc` 확인값
- `UART4.BaudRate=1000000`
- `UART4.FIFOMode=FIFOMODE_ENABLE`
- `Dma.UART4_RX`: `DMA1_Stream2`, `DMA_CIRCULAR`, `DMA_PRIORITY_VERY_HIGH`
- `PA0(TX)`: Very High, NoPull
- `PA1(RX)`: Very High, PullUp
- `NVIC1.UART4_IRQn=true`
- `NVIC1.DMA1_Stream2_IRQn=true`
